About Our Initiative

Learn about our mission and how your support helps provide critical aid to Palestinian families

Direct Support for Palestinian Families

We are a group of concerned Indian citizens collecting donations to provide direct humanitarian aid to the people of Palestine. Our mission is to channel resources to where they're needed most during this critical time.

All funds collected are used to support verified aid organizations working on the ground in Palestine, providing medical assistance, food supplies, shelter, and other essential resources to affected families.

We believe in complete transparency and will provide regular updates on how your donations are being used to make a real difference.
